Project Overview

This landing page is part of the final portfolio project developed as part of the ALX Software Engineering program. The Hostel Management System is a holistic software solution tailored for managing hostel facilities, catering to both administrative staff and residents. Our system offers an intuitive interface, ensuring efficiency in managing bookings, rooms, student profiles, messaging, and more.

Key Features

Student View

Access your profile, make new bookings, and view past bookings easily.

Staff View

Manage blocks, room types, rooms, and students. Make reservations and handle messaging.

Room Management

Efficiently manage rooms, including block and room type settings.

Student Management

Handle student-related tasks such as profiles, bookings, and communication.

Reservation System

Efficiently make, modify, or cancel reservations for students and manage booking schedules.

Messaging

Communicate and send notifications to students regarding their reservations or hostel updates.
